About Me

Leveraging 20 years of disciplined U.S. Army Special Operations experience, I am a highly adaptable and driven cybersecurity professional passionate about protecting digital assets. My academic journey at WGU, culminating in a BS and MS in Cybersecurity and Information Assurance, equipped me with practical skills in developing security policies, conducting risk assessments (NIST 800-53, PCI DSS), implementing secure cloud solutions, and performing network reconnaissance. Backed by a comprehensive suite of certifications including CompTIA Security+, Network+, CySA+, PenTest+, A+, ISC2 SSCP (Associate), EC-Council ECES, and CompTIA Project+, I excel at problem-solving under pressure and fostering team environments. I am eager to apply my unique blend of leadership, strategic thinking, and technical capabilities to an entry-level cybersecurity role and contribute to an organization's resilience.
